The Problem

There are multiple Good Country indexes. If a country is good for its people, it is good for the environment or involvement in global warming. However it is always made on what the maker says its importand. As viewer you just have to believe their statements and watch. On some indexes it is completely fine, for example the greenhouse gass emmision indexes are just numbers. However when you are making a Good Country Index you should let the viewer diside what is important. In this project The viewer can select how important he finds specific values so his own Good Country Index is made.

The Solution

In this visualisation the viewer can select how important he finds four catagories: 1) CO2 emmision per Capita 2) Waste Generation per Capita 3) Animals in the Animal Industries per Capita 4) Use of Wood resources (which contributes to deforestation in foreign countries). Depending on your index a map will be made depending on your input and your own Good Country Index will be made. Sadly not all the countries in Europe have data of the Waste Generation so not all the countries will be taken into the visualisation however when setting this value to zero the countries who do not have this data will join the visualisation.
